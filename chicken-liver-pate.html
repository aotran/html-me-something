Chicken Liver Pate

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. chicken livers
  • 0.75 lb. unsalted butter, divided
  • 0.5 cup minced onions
  • 2 Tbsp brandy
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp cracked pepper
  • 0.25 cup sour cream
  • 1 Tbsp Benedictine
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice

Preparation

Saute livers in 0.25 lb. butter. Remove livers from the skillet.

Saute onions in drippings.

Remove from heat and return livers to the skillet.

Flame brandy in small saucepan. Add to livers.

Stir in the allspice, salt, pepper, sour cream, and Benedictine.

Puree mixture until smooth.

Refrigerate for 30 minutes.

Cream remaining 0.5 lb butter until light. Add to liver mixture.

Stir in lemon juice and mix well; put into an oiled 3 to 4 cup mold, packing firmly.

Cover and refrigerate.

Allow to stand for 20 minutes before serving; garnish with parsley if desired. Serve with crackers.
